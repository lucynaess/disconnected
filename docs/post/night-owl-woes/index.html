Night owl woes

Apr 29, 2017

I thought I’d write something a bit less heavy this time. I’m a “night owl”, which if you don’t know what that means, I basically am awake mostly in the afternoon and at very late hours in the night / early morning (the opposite of most people). I also happen to live in a small town (ironically, the larget in my county), so there’s really nothing much to do at night since the town is “dead”. This coupled with the fact that a majority of my local friends are not night owls often leaves me quite lonely feeling during the night. At times I long to live somewhere a tad bigger with 24⁄7 public transporation, so if I felt like going out somewhere I easily could hop a train or bus to a bigger city and find something to do.
